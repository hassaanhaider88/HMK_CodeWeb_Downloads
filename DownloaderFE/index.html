Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Still unsure? Here are the most common questions we get.

Is HMK Downloader completely free?
Yes — 100% free, forever. There are no hidden charges, subscriptions, or premium tiers. All features are available to everyone at no cost.
Do I need to create an account?
No account, email, or sign-up required. Just open the tool and start downloading. No personal data is ever collected.
What quality are the thumbnails downloaded in?
We always fetch the highest available resolution for YouTube thumbnails — typically 1280×720 (HD) or better, depending on what the video uploader has set.
Can I download private Facebook videos?
No. HMK Downloader only works with publicly accessible content. Private, friends-only, or group-restricted videos cannot be downloaded for privacy and legal reasons.
Is it legal to download videos?
Downloading is generally acceptable for personal, offline use. Redistributing, selling, or re-uploading copyrighted content without permission is not permitted. Always respect the original creator's rights.
Why is my download not working?
Ensure the URL is from a public video and is copied correctly. Age-restricted, geo-blocked, or private content may not be supported. If the issue persists, try refreshing and re-pasting the URL.
